  • FLY is a company founded by Felix Senga and Blaise Nsenguwera – two brothers who have a vision to create opportunities for small businesses and provide convenience to customers. This vision focuses on changing how the retail and event industries work. They decided to do this by creating necessary technologies and services that have never been seen before through the unique combination of complementing industries. By doing so, they aim to foster the next generation of innovation in the retail and event sectors while increasing visibility for small to medium sized businesses. Quote from Blaise: “When we started this journey, our whole mission was to create a platform where up and coming brands can be seen and receive the recognition they deserve.” Quote from Felix: “Our focus has always been about how we can improve the customer experience and encourage collaboration within our industries. We hope that FLY can offer something different to the public and act as a catalyst for people who want to chase their creative dreams and showcase their talent.”
